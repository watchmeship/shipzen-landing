- ShipZen invoices 50% after the carrier credits the customer account.
- Existing carrier accounts, rates, representatives, and fulfillment workflow remain in place.
- UPS and FedEx support is conditional on account setup, service types, and claim paths.
- Recovery scope includes late, lost, damaged, delivered-not-received, return-to-sender, billing, duplicate-charge, dimensional-weight, and surcharge categories.
- Product-value recovery depends on claim type, carrier rules, declared value, liability limits, and evidence.
